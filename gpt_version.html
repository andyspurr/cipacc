About

CIPA-CITMA CC play friendly mid-week matches against other office-based teams. The membership is primarily patent and trademark attorneys, but there are a few ringers floating around.

Matches

We play 20 overs (light permitting) starting as close to 6pm as possible. To give everyone a game, bowlers are limited to 4 overs and you have to retire when you get to 25. There is a tea provided after the game and a chance for the teams to socialise.

Most games take place at Alexandra Palace Cricket Club. Trains travel from King's Cross, Moorgate and Finsbury Park.

Leave the station by the footbridge going West (away from the station building). Turn left on the road and follow this until you reach the park. Turn left onto the tarmac road going into the park which will lead you to the back of the cricket pavillion.
